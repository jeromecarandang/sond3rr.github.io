Welcome to Gravity Falls

Gravity Falls isn’t just any sleepy town tucked away in Oregon—it’s a place where the unexplained thrives, and secrets are hidden in plain sight. When Dipper and Mabel Pines arrive to spend the summer with their elusive Great Uncle Stan, they quickly realize there's much more beneath the surface than meets the eye. Cryptic journals, hidden messages, and strange creatures become part of their everyday life. But in Gravity Falls, the more you discover, the more questions you’re left with. Dive into the mysteries, if you dare—but remember, not everything is what it seems.






THE MYSTERY

The mystery of Gravity Falls lies in its cryptic journals, hidden secrets, and strange creatures, where nothing is ever as it seems.

THE CHARACTERS

Dipper Pines

A curious and determined boy who’s always on the hunt for hidden secrets and paranormal mysteries.

Mabel Pines

Dipper's fun-loving, energetic twin sister who brings joy and optimism to every adventure.

Grunkle Stan

The gruff yet lovable great uncle who runs the Mystery Shack, hiding a lot more than just odd souvenirs.

Soos Ramirez

The loyal, goofy handyman of the Mystery Shack, always eager to help and join in on any escapade.

Wendy Corduroy

A laid-back, cool teenager who works at the Mystery Shack and enjoys going on wild adventures with Dipper and Mabel.

Bill Cipher

A sinister, dream-manipulating demon with a triangular body who thrives on chaos and secrets.

Gideon Gleeful

A scheming child psychic obsessed with gaining power and taking over the Mystery Shack.

Ford Pines

Stan's long-lost twin brother, a genius scientist who has been battling the mysteries of Gravity Falls for decades.
