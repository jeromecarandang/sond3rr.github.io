CYBERPUNK: EDGERUNNERS

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners is a thrilling animated series set in the chaotic, neon-lit world of Cyberpunk 2077. It follows David Martinez, a rebellious street kid who, after a life-shattering event, joins a band of edgy mercenaries in Night City. With heart-pounding action, high-tech battles, and a deep dive into the dangers of cybernetic enhancements, this anime pulls you into a world where survival means embracing the chaos.
